About the City

The Pearl of
the Deccan

Hyderabad carries its history like jewels — from the soaring minarets of Charminar to the ancient walls of Golconda Fort. A city where every lane has a story centuries deep.

Known the world over for its biryani, its pearls, and its warmhearted people — this is a city that gets into your soul and never quite leaves.

📍 Madhapur, HITEC City

HITEC City — Where India Builds Tomorrow

The glass-and-steel skyline of Madhapur houses JP Morgan, AMD, Meta and hundreds of global giants. India's most concentrated tech corridor, stretching 12 km from Kukatpally to Gachibowli.

💼 Gachibowli

Insider Knowledge

Travel Tips

8 essential tips
🌤️

Best Season

October to March — cool, clear, and perfect for sightseeing. Avoid May–June heat.

🍛

Must-Eat

Hyderabadi Biryani at Bawarchi — the real deal. Non-negotiable.

🚇

Getting Around

Metro is fast, cheap and air-conditioned. Best way to cover major attractions.

☕

Local Snacks

Irani chai + Osmania biscuits every morning. Qubani ka Meetha for dessert.

✨

Evening Magic

Charminar after sunset — the illumination turns it into something ethereal.

🛍️

Shopping Tips

Bargain at Laal Bazaar — start at 50% of asking price. It's the culture.

🧴

Stay Comfortable

Sunscreen + water bottle always. Summers hit 42°C — pace yourself outside.

📅

Plan Smart

Monuments close on Fridays. Check timings in advance, plan around it.
